</Note>

## Catalog Organization

Materials within each category are sorted by the `orden` field for consistent display:

- **Materia Prima**: Alphabetical by common usage
- **Esencias**: Alphabetical A-Z
- **Varios**: Grouped by type (chemicals, dry goods, supplies)
- **Colores**: Light to dark spectrum
- **Envases Vacíos**: Small to large containers

<Tip>
  Use your browser's search (Ctrl+F / Cmd+F) when viewing order forms to quickly find specific materials.
</Tip>
